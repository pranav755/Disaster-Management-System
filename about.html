What is the Disaster Management System?

The Disaster Management System is a powerful tool designed to help individuals and communities stay informed and safe during extreme weather events and natural disasters. By providing real-time weather updates, alerts, and an interactive map, this system empowers users to make timely decisions and reduce risk.

Our Mission

Our mission is to enhance disaster preparedness and response by delivering accurate, real-time information to users. We aim to bridge the gap between emergency services and the community, ensuring safety and resilience in the face of adversity.

Features

  • Real-time weather updates.
  • Active alerts and warnings for potential hazards.
  • Interactive map for location-based insights.
  • Alert subscription for personalized notifications.
  • Easy reporting of emergencies and issues.
